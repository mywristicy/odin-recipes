Ingredients

  • White, uncooked rice of your choice.
  • Water
  • Rice Cooker

Steps

  1. First, using a measuring cup, scoop out a cup (or more) of rice into your rice cookers bowl.
  2. Next, wash the rice under water until the starch washes away. Water will appear murky white at first but will clear up after repeated rice washes.
  3. After the rice has been washed, fill up the rice bowl according to the rice cookers instructions.
  4. Place the bowl of rice and water into the rice cooker and press the start button.
  5. Wait for the rice to cook on its own. Times will vary based on amount/type of rice being cooked.
  6. After the rice has finished cooking, fluff the rice in the bowl by moving it around gently to prevent the rice from sticking to the walls of the bowl.
  7. Serve in a bowl or plate, eat, enjoy.
