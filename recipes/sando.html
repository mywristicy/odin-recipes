Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Protein
  • Spread (Mayo, Mustard, etc)
  • Vegatables (Lettuce, Tomamto, etc)
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Grab two slices of bread, assuming your bread is sliced.
  2. Apply choice of spread evenly to both sides.
  3. Prepare your protein, in this case, sliced turkey.
  4. Add desired amounts of sliced turkey(protein) to one slice of your bread.
  5. Prepare your Vegatables however you prefer. I will slice my tomatoes and break apart lettuce into manageable leaves.
  6. Add your desired amount of Vegatables to the same slice that holds the protein.
  7. Prepare your cheese, if desired, not necessary.
  8. Add your desired amount of cheese to the bread slice holding the Vegatables and protein.
  9. Place the other bread slice over the mound of ingredients to "sandwich" the contents between the two slices of bread.
  10. Eat, enjoy.
