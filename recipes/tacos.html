Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Choice of Protein
  • Onion
  • Cliantro
  • Limes
  • Cooking oil of choice

Steps

  1. First, prep your protein. Season it or whatever.
  2. Next, heat a pan to a good hot temp. You decide what "good hot" is.
  3. Cook protein till cooked.
  4. Heat tortillas.
  5. Dice onions, chop cliantro.
  6. Place cooked protein inside of a tortilla. Place a reasonable amount of onion and cliantro on top of the protein.
  7. Cut a lime in half. Squeeze said cut lime to expel juice from the citrus onto the assembled taco.
  8. If available, add your choice of salsa/sauce.
  9. Eat, enjoy.
